Privacy notice

Last updated: June 21, 2026

1. Who we are

Fall Guardian is operated by Thomas Laure ("we", "us", "our"). For questions about this notice or your personal data, contact us at thomas.laure@enovacom.com.

Data controller: Thomas Laure — thomas.laure@enovacom.com

2. What data we collect

When you submit the waitlist form on our website, we may collect:

  • First and/or last name (optional, if you choose to provide it)
  • Email address (required — used to contact you about the private preview)
  • Your role or relationship to Fall Guardian (optional — e.g. "Family caregiver")
  • A free-text message (optional — any context or question you share)
  • Consent status and the date on which consent was given
  • Submission timestamp and basic anti-spam metadata (e.g. form submission token)

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health data, financial data, government identifiers) through this form.

3. Why we collect it and on what legal basis

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Replying to your request — to respond to any question or message you submitted. Legal basis: legitimate interest (responding to a direct inquiry).
  • Managing private preview access — to contact you when a spot opens in the Fall Guardian private preview. Legal basis: your explicit consent, given via the consent checkbox on the form.
  • Sending product updates — to notify you of developments related to Fall Guardian, such as the public launch. Legal basis: your explicit consent.

You may withdraw your consent at any time (see Section 6). Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing that took place before withdrawal.

4. Processors and third-party services

To operate Fall Guardian and manage waitlist data, we may use the following processors. Each processes your data solely on our instructions and in accordance with a data processing agreement:

  • Alwaysdata — web hosting provider, located in France (EU). The landing page and any static assets are served from Alwaysdata infrastructure.
  • Tally — form collection service, if used as a form back-end. Data may be stored on Tally servers. See Tally's privacy policy.
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — email and contact management platform, used to manage waitlist contacts and send preview invitations. See Brevo's privacy policy.
  • Matomo — self-hosted web analytics, if analytics are enabled. See Section 8 on cookies.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. How long we keep your data

We retain waitlist data until you withdraw your consent or for a maximum of 24 months after our last meaningful contact with you — whichever comes first. After this period, your data will be deleted or anonymised.

If you are admitted to the private preview and become an active user, a separate in-app privacy notice will apply to data collected through the Fall Guardian application.

6. Your rights under the GDPR

As a data subject in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — you may ask us to delete your personal data, subject to applicable legal exceptions.
  • Right to restriction of processing — you may ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to object — you may object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at thomas.laure@enovacom.com. We will respond within one month.

7. Complaints

If you believe we are not handling your data lawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the French supervisory authority:

CNIL — Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés

8. Cookies and analytics

By default, the Fall Guardian landing page does not set any analytics or tracking cookies. No third-party advertising trackers are present.

If we enable self-hosted Matomo analytics in the future, it will be configured to anonymise IP addresses and avoid cross-site tracking. A consent banner will be added before any non-essential cookies are set, in accordance with CNIL guidelines.

9. Changes to this notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be communicated to waitlist members by email.
